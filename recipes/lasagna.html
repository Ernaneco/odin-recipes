Lasagna Bolognese Recipe

Let's learn a delicious lasagna bolognese recipe

Ingredients

  • 500 grams of ground beef
  • 500 grams of dry pasta
  • chopped onion
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 can of peeled tomatoes
  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Melt the butter in the pan and add the onion to saut√©
  2. Add the ground beef and season with salt and black pepper
  3. Add the milk and mix until it evaporates completely
  4. Lower the heat and add the peeled tomatoes
  5. Preheat the oven
  6. Assemble the lasagna layers in an ovenproof dish
  7. Add grated parmesan cheese and bake in the oven for about 15 minutes
